# Jewathu LPLUDP
- Jewathu Centre is a commercial and residential centre, 
- located in Njoro Sub-County, at the intersection of the c56, Njoro Road, and C57, Molo Road.
- Situated 18 kilometres south0-west of Nakuru City.

- Started out as a scheme by the defunct County Council of Nakuru. To settle the elderly and landless members of the community.
